WhatsApp Emoji Final Interaction Build

Final Interaction Details

Triggers

  • Clicking on the blank face icon
  • Clicking the blank face icon is also the way to close the menu
  • Hovering on/off the blank face emoji
  • Hovering on/off the emojis themselves
  • Click outside of the interaction radius to close it

Rules

Front-end

  • The emojis stay up until you've used one of them (assuming it functioned programming wise) or clicked the blank face icon. It closes the same way as it appears but in reverse.
  • The users five most commonly used emojis are displayed, lined up horizontally with 3px spacing from one another, after clicking the blank face icon. The most used emoji appears as the left-most emoji and the least most commonly used emoji appears as the right-most emoji.
  • The emojis appear in less than a second and stay at 35% opacity until a user hovers over them. Then, within a quarter of a second, the emoji hovered over fills to 100% opacity.
  • The cursor changes to a pointer when hovering over the the blank face icon that was clicked on to have the emojis appear. It also changes to a pointer when the emojis are hovered over.
  • A grey circle appears behind the blank face icon while it is being hovered over. The grey circle remains visible as long as the interaction is active.
  • Hovering over any of the users 5 favorite emojis results in a one second animation of the hovered over emoji rising and fading into place at the same time. The fade-in animation starts at 59% opacity and ends at 100% opacity. The rise begins at 0px and ends after having moved 10px vertically. All of this happens within 1 second. When they're hovered off of, the reverse of the interaction happens with an additional bounce animation as the emoji reaches it original position. It taps said position then bounces 1 pixel upwards before settling into place.
  • Once the animation ends, the emoji remain at 100% opacity and 10px raised vertically for however long the user remains howevered over that emoji.
  • Multiple emojis can have their animations begin and end at the same time.
  • Clicking the blank face icon results in a one second animation of the users top 5 favorite emojis fading into place. The emoji fade-in animation starts at 59% opacity and ends at 100% opacity.
  • Once the animation ends, the emojis remain at 100% opacity for 1 second before instantly changing back to 59% opacity, all at the same time.
  • When the blank face icon is hovered over, it increases 15% in size in a swell-like animation. When hovered off of, it shrinks by 15% in the same style of animation that occured for enlarging.

Back-end

  • Database has to store and retrieve users most frequently used emojis for their top 5 'favorites' to be shown in the emoji menu.

Loops & Modes

  • Several different emojis appear which have their opacity increase from 59% to 100% by way of hover.
  • Upon activating the microinteraction, a one second animation of hovered over emoji rising occurs, starting at 59% opacity and ending at 100% opacity. It rises a total of 10px vertically. The reverse animation occurs when it is hovered off of.
  • Upon activating the microinteraction, a one second animation of all of the emojis rolling into place occurs, at 59% opacity from the start to the end.
